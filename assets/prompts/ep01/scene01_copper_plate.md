The copper surface is dark with centuries of patina — deep brown-green oxidation in the recesses, warm bronze highlights on raised surfaces. Photorealistic, macro photography style. Warm color palette: Temple Stone amber-brown, Chola Bronze, dark patina. No shiny new copper. The plate looks ancient, handled, heavy. Visible surface texture and micro-shadow detail in every groove and scratch.
